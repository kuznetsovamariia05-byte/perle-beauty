Bruno-Marek-Allee, 1020 Wien, Austria.
Target audience: affluent, style-conscious women and men in Vienna who value exclusivity, quality, and premium service.
The design must feel:
* Elegant
* Refined
* Calm
* Expensive
* Modern luxury
* Minimalistic
Avoid overly decorative elements. Focus on typography, spacing and refined color palette.
Brand colors:
* Background: #F9F8F7
* Main text: #1C1C1C
* Accent tones: #9A958E, #C2B19C, #63402C
* Use subtle beige textures or soft stone-like background.
* Gold or warm brown accents for buttons.
Typography:
* Headings: Bebas Neue (or elegant luxury serif alternative)
* Body: Lato or clean modern sans-serif
The website must be bilingual (English / German) with a visible language switcher in the header.

Required Sections
1. Hero Section
Large refined headline: “Perle – Luxury Beauty Atelier in Vienna”
Subheadline: Premium nail artistry, brows & lashes for a flawless look.
CTA button: “Book Your Appointment”

2. Services & Pricing (Luxury Segment Pricing)
Nail Services:
* Signature Women’s Manicure – €65
* Gentleman’s Manicure – €55
* French Couture Manicure – €75
* Luxury Gel Manicure – €85
* BIAB / Builder Gel – €95
* Gel Removal – €25
Brows & Lashes:
* Brow Shaping & Styling – €35
* Brow Lamination – €75
* Lash Lift – €95
* Lash Lift + Tint – €110
* Lash Tint – €35
Display services in elegant cards with premium spacing.

3. Online Booking System
Include a luxury-style booking interface:
* Service selection
* Add-ons
* Date picker
* Time selection
* Client details
* Confirmation page
Design should feel seamless, calm and exclusive.

4. Location Section
Show: Bruno-Marek-Allee, 1020 Wien, Austria
Include buttons:
* “Open in Google Maps”
* “Open in Apple Maps”
Embedded map preview with minimal frame.

5. About Section
Short refined text about:
* High-end products
* Hygiene standards
* Personalised service
* Relaxing atmosphere
* Attention to detail
Tone: sophisticated and calm.

6. Footer
Include:
* Opening hours
* Contact info
* Instagram
* Language switch
* Privacy policy

Design Guidelines:
* Lots of white space
* Smooth animations
* Rounded elegant buttons
* Subtle shadows
* Premium UX
* Mobile-first responsive
* Clean navigation
